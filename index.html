Why CS & Deep Learning

CS rewards clear thinking and ships real tools. Deep learning blends elegant math with gritty engineering. I love decomposing big problems—like optimization under uncertainty—into parts I can test, visualize, and ship. The harder the problem, the more fun it is to make the solution legible.

Principles

  • Start simple → iterate. Build the smallest honest baseline, then improve with evidence.
  • Reproduce everything. Scripts, seeds, and configs live with the code. Plots are programmatic.
  • Explain the trade-offs. Users should see why a route/model was chosen—not just the output.
  • Keep it legible. Code, docs, and UX should make future work easier, not harder.
