My Principles (How I Work)
  1. 01 Start simple. Build the smallest honest baseline and improve it with evidence.
  2. 02 Reproduce everything. Keep scripts, seeds, configs, and assumptions with the code.
  3. 03 Engineer for scale. Use clear APIs, tests, and observability.
  4. 04 Explain the tradeoffs. Show why a route or model was chosen.
  5. 05 Keep it legible. Make code, docs, and UX easy to extend.
