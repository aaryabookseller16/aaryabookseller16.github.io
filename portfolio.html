NaviAI — Maritime Routing with Risk

Founder & Lead Engineer · 2025–Present

Multi-objective routing that balances time, fuel, and operational risk. The engine fuses live weather and port conditions with a news-derived risk score (Transformer sentiment + rules) to adjust edge weights in real time. Constraints like draft limits, traffic separation schemes, exclusion zones, and canal fees are modeled explicitly.

A Streamlit UI shows fastest / cheapest / safest alternatives with deltas in ETA, fuel burn, and risk. Each route includes short LLM rationales. The pipeline includes cached data fetchers, reproducible configs, and scenario “playbacks” for incident post-mortems and what-if planning.

Contract Theory — Implicit-Gradient Bilevel

Deep Learning Research Group · Texas A&M

Principal–agent contract design posed as a bilevel program: the principal chooses contract parameters, the agent best-responds with effort, and the outer objective optimizes principal utility. We compute outer gradients via implicit differentiation and Hessian–vector products solved with conjugate gradient, avoiding explicit Hessians.

Training stability comes from warm-starts, damping, and line-searched updates; baselines include grid search and straight-through approximations. Experiments cover synthetic PA setups (noise, incentive compatibility, effort-cost regimes), with ablations on schedule choice and regularization, plus reproducible figures and tables.

Style Converter — VGG19 NST

Simple, clean neural style transfer demo

Classic VGG19 neural style transfer with a streamlined UI: upload content & style, then tune content/style weights, TV loss, and iteration budget. Uses a coarse→fine schedule and multi-scale Gram features for crisp textures without over-sharpening.

Includes deterministic seeds, progress snapshots, and side-by-side previews. Runs on CPU or GPU; large images are tiled and reassembled to keep memory bounded and results reproducible across devices.
