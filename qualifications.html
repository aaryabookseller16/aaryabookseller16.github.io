Publications

Gradient-Based Bilevel Optimization for Principal–Agent Contract Design

A. Bookseller, T. Galanti, K. Ray · Under Review (NeurIPS 2025)

Presents an implicit-differentiation approach for economic contract design problems formulated as bilevel programs. The method uses Hessian-vector products and conjugate gradient to scale outer-loop updates without explicit Hessians. Empirical studies include grid-search baselines, ablations on learning-rate schedules, and convergence diagnostics.

  • Contributions: practical implicit-gradient solver, stability heuristics, and evaluation protocol.
  • Artifacts: reproducible PyTorch code, logging utilities, and publication-quality figures.
